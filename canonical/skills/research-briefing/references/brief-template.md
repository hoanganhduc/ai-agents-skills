# Research Brief Template

Use this exact shape when a compact visible brief helps:

```text
Research Brief
- Goal: ...
- Scope: ...
- Constraints: ...
- Context/style artifacts: ...
- Evidence plan: ...
- Formal (optional): off | mention-only | intake-if-lemma-stable
- Workflow: ...
- Risks: ...
```

Keep each line short. Prefer concrete source classes such as official docs, papers, standards, or vendor documentation.

When `Formal` is not `off`, name the handoff chain (`lean-formalization-intake` → skeleton → optional `opengauss` → `lean-strict-verification-gate`). Do not promise “research will prove it in Lean.”
