# Report Contract

`recommendation.md` must include:

- scope and inspected inputs
- delivery status
- ranked venue shortlist only when delivery status is `ready`
- provisional candidate venues when comparator evidence is missing
- comparator evidence matrix with provider, source/query IDs, year window,
  evidence level, and evidence IDs
- per-venue evidence summary
- score components with caveats
- unsupported or stale evidence gaps
- Review Findings
- Delivery Check

Separate observations from inferred fit. Include `incomplete analysis` when
material evidence is missing or provider coverage is blocked.

`incomplete analysis` must appear near the top of the report when any ranked
venue lacks comparator-paper evidence. Bibliography overlap, reputation, and
offline placeholders are discovery signals only and must not be presented as a
deliverable ranked shortlist.

Do not include raw draft text by default.
